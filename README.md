# ⚖️ BMI Calculator — Know Your Health!

> "Your health is your wealth!" 💪

---

## 🚀 What it does
Calculate your Body Mass Index instantly!

---

## 📖 How to use
1. 🏋️ Enter weight in kg
2. 📏 Enter height in cm
3. 🖱️ Click Calculate BMI!

---

## 📊 Categories
- 🥺 Underweight → BMI less than 18.5
- ✅ Normal → BMI 18.5 to 24.9
- ⚠️ Overweight → BMI 25 to 29.9
- 🚨 Obese → BMI 30 and above
